Bollywood Movie Reviews

1. Animal (2023)

Director: Sandeep Reddy Vanga

Cast: Ranbir Kapoor, Rashmika Mandanna, Anil Kapoor

Review: A gripping, intense action-drama that explores complex father-son dynamics. Ranbir delivers a power-packed performance.

Rating: ★★★★☆

2. Jawan (2023)

Director: Atlee

Cast: Shah Rukh Khan, Nayanthara, Vijay Sethupathi

Review: High on action and emotion, Jawan is a mass entertainer with SRK in full form. Great music and visuals!

Rating: ★★★★☆

3. Rocky Aur Rani Kii Prem Kahaani (2023)

Director: Karan Johar

Cast: Ranveer Singh, Alia Bhatt

Review: A vibrant love story packed with drama, culture clashes, and lots of heart. Ranveer and Alia shine throughout.

Rating: ★★★★☆
